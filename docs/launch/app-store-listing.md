## Full description
Cashenza custom-bundle helps merchants turn a standard product page into a more effective bundle offer experience.

With Cashenza, you can create bundle ladders such as 1 pack, 2 pack, 3 pack, or fully custom multi-item offers, then style the storefront block directly from Shopify. Merchants can highlight a best seller offer, show savings badges, add urgency with a timer, and let customers choose variants for each item inside the selected bundle.

The app is built for stores that want a cleaner and faster alternative to heavy bundle apps. Instead of overwhelming the merchant with unnecessary complexity, Cashenza focuses on the bundle controls that matter most for conversion:

- Per-item variant selection inside the chosen offer
- Optional variant thumbnails
- Best seller badges and savings badges
- Multiple design presets
- Configurable colors, spacing, and border radius
- Percentage discounts, fixed amount discounts, and fixed price offers
- Shopify-native automatic discount sync

Cashenza custom-bundle is especially well suited for merchants selling products where color, size, scent, finish, or visual choice matters inside the bundle flow.

## FAQ
### Does the app support variant selection inside bundles?
Yes. Customers can select variants for each item included in the active bundle offer.

### Can I show thumbnails for variants?
Yes. Variant thumbnails can be enabled when you want a more visual bundle experience.

### Can I highlight one offer as the recommended option?
Yes. You can mark one offer as the best seller and style the badge colors.

### What discount types are supported?
Cashenza supports percentage discounts, fixed amount discounts, and fixed price offers.

### Does the app work with Shopify discounts?
Yes. The app syncs bundle discounts using Shopify-native billing and discount flows.

### Can I customize the design?
Yes. Merchants can change colors, spacing, border radius, badge styles, text, and design presets from Shopify.

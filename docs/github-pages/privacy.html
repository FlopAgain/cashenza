Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 20, 2026

Cashenza custom-bundle, operated by CASHENZA ("we", "us", "our"), provides a Shopify app that helps merchants create configurable product bundle offers on their Shopify storefronts.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share personal information when merchants install or use Cashenza custom-bundle, and how we process limited buyer-related information as part of providing the app.

1. Who we collect information from

We may collect information from:

  • merchants who install or use Cashenza custom-bundle
  • users acting on behalf of those merchants
  • buyers, only to the limited extent necessary to provide app functionality through Shopify

2. Information we collect

Merchant and store information

When a merchant installs or uses Cashenza custom-bundle, we may collect:

  • store domain
  • Shopify shop identifier
  • app session and authentication information
  • bundle configuration data created in the app
  • app settings and preferences
  • billing and subscription status related to the app
  • technical usage information, logs, and diagnostics

Buyer-related information

We do not collect buyer information for unrelated marketing purposes. However, in order to provide app functionality, we may process limited data made available through Shopify, such as:

  • product selections related to configured bundles
  • variant and cart-related bundle metadata
  • technical information needed to apply or support bundle discount behavior

We only process buyer-related information to the extent necessary to operate, secure, support, and improve the app.

3. How we use information

We use personal information to:

  • provide and operate Cashenza custom-bundle
  • authenticate merchants and maintain app sessions
  • store merchant bundle settings and configuration
  • deliver storefront bundle functionality
  • support bundle discount logic and related app behavior
  • provide billing, account administration, and customer support
  • monitor performance, diagnose errors, and maintain security
  • improve app features and merchant experience
  • comply with legal obligations

4. Shopify and third-party service providers

Our app is made available to merchants who use Shopify. Merchant stores are hosted by Shopify, and Shopify may process personal information when merchants and buyers use Shopify-powered storefronts and services.

Information submitted through a Shopify store or through this app may be shared with Shopify and with third-party service providers used to operate the app, including providers for hosting, database infrastructure, analytics, logging, support, and billing-related operations.

For more information about how Shopify processes personal information, please review:

  • Shopify Privacy Policy
  • Shopify privacy choices

5. Legal bases and privacy rights

Depending on the jurisdiction involved, we may process personal information to perform our contract with the merchant, where necessary for our legitimate interests in operating and improving the app, to comply with legal obligations, or based on consent where required by law.

Depending on applicable law, individuals may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, portability, restriction, objection, or withdrawal of consent.

Requests may be sent to: cashenza@outlook.com

6. Data retention

We retain information for as long as necessary to provide the app to merchants, maintain business and billing records, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and comply with legal obligations.

If a merchant uninstalls the app, we may delete or anonymize data after a reasonable retention period, unless longer retention is required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or legitimate business purposes.

7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

8. International transfers

Personal information may be processed and stored in countries outside the merchant’s or buyer’s jurisdiction, including where our infrastructure providers or service providers operate.

9. Children’s data

Cashenza custom-bundle is intended for use by businesses and is not directed to children.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above and, where appropriate, provide additional notice.
